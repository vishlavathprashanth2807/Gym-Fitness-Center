Fitness Calculator

Welcome to the MBNR Fitness Calculator! Our calculator helps you understand your body's fitness level by calculating your Body Mass Index (BMI). Simply enter your height and weight to find out whether you are underweight, have a healthy weight, are overweight, or fall into the obesity category.
BMI is a widely used health indicator that helps estimate whether your weight is appropriate for your height. While it does not directly measure body fat, it serves as a useful starting point for tracking your fitness journey.

How to Use..

  • Enter your height in centimeters (cm).
  • Enter your weight in kilograms (kg).
  • Click the Calculate BMI button.
  • Your BMI value and health category will be displayed instantly.
  • Can be Calculated using the following formula
    • height(cm) = height/100
    • BMI = weight(kg)/(height*height)

BMI Categories

BMI Category
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 – 24.9 Normal Weight
25.0 – 29.9 Overweight
30.0 and Above Obese
